Hey! So we have two analytics pages

one at /activity, which is restricted to is_staff (as you noted)

that has info about all the realms, and should basically only be accessible to the server admin. In the dev environment, I think shylock has is_staff=True. (You can get shylock by running ./manage.py populate_analytics_db.py, added just a week or two ago)

the other page is /stats, which will be accessible to all users, and has information about themselves and their realm. Amy is working on that, hopefully we'll have something out in the next couple of days.

There are also two more pages in the analytics roadmap, that haven't been started yet:

  • A page for realm admins with info about all the users in their realm (e.g. who is active, who is not active)
  • Something for zulip.org, with aggregate info about all zulip installations that choose to share that info with us (basic stuff like #users, #messages, #installations, etc)
